# Court Ready Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Court Ready ("the app", "we", "our") handles your information. Court Ready is built and operated by Adrianne Harlow as an independent developer.

We've kept this short and direct because the data picture is genuinely simple: we collect what we need to run the app for you, we don't sell anything, and we don't track you across other apps or websites.

---

## 1. What Court Ready Is

Court Ready is a strength and conditioning tracking app for coaches and athletes. You add athletes to your roster, build workouts, run training sessions, and track progress over time.

The app stores your data in two places:

- **On your device** — using your phone's local storage. This works without an account.
- **In your account in the cloud** — if you choose to sign in. Cloud storage is provided through Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore), which acts as our service provider.

You can use the app without creating an account. Creating an account enables sync across devices.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### When you create an account

- **Email address** — required for account creation and sign-in
- **Display name** — only if you provide one, or if it's returned by Sign in with Apple or Google sign-in on first authentication
- **Account identifiers** — a unique user ID generated by Firebase Authentication, used internally to associate your data with your account

We do not ask for or collect:
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Date of birth
- Payment information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store; we never see card or banking data)
- Photos, contacts, or any data outside the app
- Location data
- Health or fitness data from Apple Health or any other health platform

### When you use the app

We store the content you create:

- **Athlete profiles** you add (name, position, class, optional notes)
- **Workouts** you build (exercise selection, sets, reps, weight targets)
- **Sessions** you log (which exercises were performed, sets completed, reps, weight, time, RPE, dates)
- **Custom exercises** you add to your library
- **Assignments** you create

This information is stored in your account and is only accessible to you when signed in. If you don't create an account, this content stays only on your device.

### When you use voice features

If you tap the microphone button to use voice commands during a session, the app uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. **Audio is processed on your iPhone and is not sent to us, to Anthropic, to Google, or to any other server.** We do not record, store, or transmit any audio.

If you grant microphone and speech recognition permissions and later change your mind, you can revoke them anytime in iOS Settings → Court Ready.

### Information we don't collect

We don't run analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, or any third-party telemetry inside Court Ready. We don't know how often you open the app, which screens you visit, or what device you're on, beyond what Firebase Authentication needs to verify your identity at sign-in.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above only to:

- Create and authenticate your account
- Save your roster, workouts, and session history so you can access it across devices
- Calculate your progress charts and training statistics inside the app
- Respond to support questions if you email us

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, lookalike audiences, or any purpose unrelated to running the app for you.

---

## 4. Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

We share information with these service providers, and only to the extent needed to run the app:

- **Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore)** — stores your account credentials and app data. Subject to Google's terms and privacy policies.
- **Apple** — when you use Sign in with Apple, Apple authenticates you and provides us a unique identifier and (on first sign-in) optionally your name and email. Subject to Apple's privacy policies.

We may disclose information if legally required (subpoena, court order, or to protect against fraud or imminent harm). We have not received any such requests as of the effective date above.

---

## 5. Where Your Data Lives

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, primarily in the United States. If you're outside the US, your data is processed in the US under Google's standard data processing agreements.

Data on your device stays on your device until you sign in to an account or delete it.

---

## 6. How Long We Keep It

We keep your account data as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or contact us asking us to delete your data, we remove it from our active systems within 30 days. Backups age out within 90 days.

---

## 7. Your Rights and Controls

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights with your data in Court Ready:

- **See what we have** — email us and we'll send you a copy
- **Correct it** — most data you can edit directly in the app; for anything else, email us
- **Delete it** — you can delete athletes, workouts, and sessions yourself in the app, or email us to delete your entire account
- **Take it elsewhere** — email us and we'll send your data in a portable format (JSON)
- **Withdraw consent** — stop using the app and email us to delete your account

If you live in **California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, Texas, or any state with comprehensive privacy laws**, you have these same rights under those laws. We do not sell personal information and do not engage in "sharing" or "targeted advertising" as defined by those laws, so opt-out rights for those activities are not applicable.

If you live in the **EU, UK, or EEA**, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. The legal basis for processing your account data is performance of a contract (you using the app); for any other processing, it's legitimate interest in operating the app.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address in Section 11.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Court Ready is designed for coaches and adult athletes. The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

**If you're a coach using Court Ready to track minor athletes:** the data you enter (athlete names, positions, performance data) is content you control. You're responsible for any consents required by your school, club, or jurisdiction to collect and store performance data on minors. Court Ready stores this data on your behalf as a tool, similar to a coach's notebook.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account with us, email us and we'll delete it immediately.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data:

- All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
- Firebase enforces authentication checks on every read and write — only you can access your own data
- Passwords are hashed and salted by Firebase Authentication; we never see your raw password
- Sign in with Apple and Google sign-in use OAuth and don't transmit passwords to us at all

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever experience a breach involving your data, we'll notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of it, in accordance with applicable laws.
